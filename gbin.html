Projects / Gbin

Product

Gbin is a smart bin designed for recycling. This system can identify the type of garbage and sort it automatically. Gbin is conceived for restaurants kitchen, to help workers to optimize time.
The project is a Master's Thesis.
Download pdf

Personas

Gbin is designed and conceived for two type of users:

Anna

25-35 years old, Chef.
Low-middle level of instruction, quite ecologist.
She trie sorting wastes, but because of quicly pace of work, sometimes she makes some mistakes.

Mario

50-70 years old, Manager.
Low-middle level of instruction, quite ecologist.
He does sorting in his restaurant, but sometimes his workers make mistakes and he has to pay a penalty.

Prototype

The user throws the garbage into the smart bin. The sensors inside the bin detect and identify the type of garbage (plastic, glass, organic, paper), then an automated arm takes the waste one by one and sort it in the correct bin inside the gbin. It also detect and notify the fill level of each internal bin. The smart bin can have different dimension and color according to the restaurant preferences.
