Projects / My Care

Product

My Care is an health care monitoring system designed for elder people. A smart bracelet monitors the health condition of the user and send the information to the mobile app. In this way, any member of the family can monitor the health condition of that person. If the bracelet feels that something is going wrong, it sends an alarm to the app and the relative can call for help.
Download pdf

Personas

My Care is designed and conceived for two type of users:

Alberto

71 years old, retired
High-level of technological knowledge
He usually take care of his health conditions

Claudio

51 years old, salesman
He works all day and lives away from his mother, who suffers from several illnesses and can't be left alone. He would like to see her more often

Prototype

The system is composed by a smart bracelet for the elder user and a mobile app that monitors the health condition in real time.
